RUCHESS roo · chess Rust · Universal · Chess
Runs everywhere — from datacenter racks to a Raspberry Pi in the corner of a café. The piece system follows: six sets, one universal silhouette grammar.

Six original chess sets for the Ruchess platform — drawn as SVG so they ship over the wire at the size of a thought and render crisp on every screen we deploy to. Each set carries its own material story, its own palette, and its own answer to the question what does chess look like here? // piece-system.svg · v1.0 · may 2026
